# Railway Deployment Guide

## Quick Deploy to Railway

1. **Sign up at Railway**: https://railway.app
2. **Connect GitHub**: Link your repository
3. **Deploy**: Railway auto-detects Docker and deploys

## What Railway Provides:
- ✅ Permanent public URL (like `https://your-app.railway.app`)
- ✅ Automatic HTTPS
- ✅ Database hosting
- ✅ Background workers (Celery)
- ✅ Free tier available

## Alternative Platforms:
- **Render**: https://render.com (similar to Railway)
- **Heroku**: https://heroku.com (more expensive)
- **DigitalOcean App Platform**: https://cloud.digitalocean.com/apps

## Cost:
- Railway: Free tier (500 hours/month)
- Render: Free tier (750 hours/month)
- Heroku: $7/month minimum
